29 promoted bundles · 3-frame windows

MuSiP traffic analyzer wave reports

This root serves every promoted waveform bundle in tb_int/wave_reports/. Each case has a checked-in VCD, an opq.svd snapshot, bundle metadata, and a local packet analyzer page that correlates ingress, merged OPQ egress, and DMA across a 3-frame display window.

The masked-lane BASIC cases intentionally keep all four ingress lanes visible in the VCD. The single surviving lane is identified by the case contract, while the analyzer still shows the pre-mask traffic on every captured lane for correlation.

Long profile captures keep the full simulation depth in bundle.json and display the middle three frames by default; P123 and P124 currently serve F6..F8 from their 16-frame runs.
